VIP All Inclusive Guided Day Trip — Giza Pyramids, Sakkara & Memphis

Private luxury vehicle, licensed Egyptologist guide, camel ride at Giza, and an authentic Egyptian lunch. Hotel pickup & drop-off included.

Overview

Duration: Full day (approx. 8–10 hours) • Start time: 8:00 AM (flexible) • Pickup: Cairo hotels / Nile-side hotels / Giza area

This VIP private tour visits the highlights of Greater Giza and Old Kingdom necropoleis: the Great Pyramids & Sphinx, a camel ride near the pyramids, then on to Sakkara (Step Pyramid of Djoser), and Memphis (the ancient capital) — with an expert Egyptologist guide and a delicious lunch at a local high-quality restaurant.

Itinerary

08:00 — Hotel pickup

Our driver and guide pick you up from your hotel in Cairo or Giza. Short briefing and drive to the Giza Plateau.

09:00 — Giza Pyramids & Sphinx

Private guided visit to the Great Pyramid of Khufu area, Khafre, and Menkaure. Walk around the plateau, explanation of the tombs and funerary complexes. Optional inside-pyramid entry (extra ticket).

  • Photo stops with the Pyramids
  • Visit the Sphinx and Valley Temple area
  • Optional Pyramid interior entry (not included)
11:30 — Camel ride experience

Short guided camel ride near the Pyramids (approx. 20–30 minutes) with photo opportunities.

12:30 — Lunch

Enjoy a VIP-style lunch at a selected restaurant with local cuisine (vegetarian options available).

14:00 — Sakkara (Step Pyramid of Djoser)

Explore the Step Pyramid complex, learn about Old Kingdom funerary practices and see tomb reliefs and mastabas.

16:00 — Memphis

Short visit to the open-air museum of ancient Memphis: Colossal statue of Ramses II, sphinxes, and artifacts from the Old Kingdom.

17:00 — Return to hotel

Comfortable drive back to your hotel. Drop-off in Cairo or Giza.

Includes

  • Private licensed Egyptologist guide
  • Air-conditioned private vehicle with driver
