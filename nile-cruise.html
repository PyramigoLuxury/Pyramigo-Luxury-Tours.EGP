Tour Description

Enjoy a combination of Egyptian food, traditional dances, a sailing trip and views to the illuminated city of Cairo on this Nile River dinner cruise.

You will be picked up from your hotel by your private driver and representative and taken to the port on the Nile River. Board the luxurious boat and enjoy a few hours of wonderful Egyptian food, mesmerizing belly dancers and whirling Dervish (Tanoura) show, enchanting music, live band and a lovely breeze.

Take pictures of the show and of Cairo's stunning skyline from the upper deck. After the cruise, you’ll be transferred back to your hotel.

What's Included

  • Hotel pickup and drop-off (Cairo or Giza)
  • Open buffet dinner on the Nile
  • Live belly dance & Tanoura show
  • Private air-conditioned vehicle
  • English-speaking guide

Departure Details

  • Departure Point: Your hotel in Cairo or Giza
  • Departure Time: 6:00 PM
  • Duration: Around 4 hours
